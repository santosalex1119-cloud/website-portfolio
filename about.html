Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Alexander Santos Castillo, an Agricultural Communications & Journalism student at Texas A&M University with a passion for storytelling, graphic design, public relations, and digital media.

My goal is to create communications that educate, inspire, and connect people through visual design, strategic messaging, and creative problem solving.

Throughout college I've developed projects in Adobe Creative Cloud, branding, publication design, event promotion, social media, and web design while continuing to build my communication skills through classroom and real-world experiences.

Career Goals

I am passionate about building a career where communication, creativity, and service come together. My goal is to work in communications, public affairs, media relations, or agricultural advocacy, helping organizations tell meaningful stories and connect with the communities they serve. Through continued professional experience, I hope to strengthen my skills in graphic design, digital media, event communications, and public engagement. Looking ahead, I plan to pursue law school to expand my knowledge of policy, advocacy, and leadership while continuing to make a positive impact through strategic communication.

Beyond the Classroom

Outside of academics, I enjoy pursuing interests that encourage creativity, collaboration, and personal growth. Sports have always been an important part of my life, and I enjoy following soccer, baseball, basketball, and football. I also enjoy graphic design, photography, and web development, where I can continue developing creative and technical skills beyond the classroom. I value opportunities to apply what I learn through volunteer work, professional experiences, and community involvement. These experiences have strengthened my communication, teamwork, adaptability, and problem-solving skills while reinforcing my passion for working with others and contributing to meaningful projects. Whether I'm designing a publication, attending a sporting event, or building a new website, I enjoy challenging myself to think creatively, communicate effectively, and continue growing both personally and professionally. I believe that maintaining a balance between academics, personal interests, and hands-on experiences has helped shape me into a well-rounded student and aspiring communications professional. As I continue my journey at Texas A&M University, I look forward to embracing new opportunities that allow me to learn, create, and make a positive impact both on campus and in the communities I serve.
